#+title: TLS

#+startup: overview

1. Handshake communication
   1. Browser asks to communicate over TLS
   2. the server sends a over encryption algorithm (cipher) and its certificate
   3. Browser verifies certificate with CA (certificate authority)
   4. they agree on encryption keys (asy)
   5. data is exchanged with symmetric shared secret (**AES - Advanced Encryption Standard*)
